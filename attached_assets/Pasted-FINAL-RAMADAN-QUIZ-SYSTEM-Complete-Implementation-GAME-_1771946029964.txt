**Phase 2: Entry Phase (1 Minute)**
- All 6 teams allowed to join during 1-minute window

- Automatically close entry after 1 minute expires
- Show "Entry Closed" message after deadline

**Phase 3: Turn Management System**
- Sequential team rotation (Team 1 → Team 2 → Team 3 → etc.)
- Within each team: one player answers at a time, then next player
- Non-active teams see "Waiting for your turn" screen
- Clear indication of which team/player is currently active

**Phase 4: Question Selection (1 Minute)**
- Active player sees only their assigned 6 question numbers (not all 31)
- Player selects one number from their personal allocation
- System displays the question associated with that number
- Selected questions become unavailable for all other players
- 1-minute countdown for selection
- Example: Player assigned numbers [3,7,12,18,25,29] → picks #12 → Question 12 appears

**Phase 5: Preparation Time (30 Seconds)**
- Question displays on main screen for audience
- Active player gets 30 seconds to read and prepare
- Show countdown timer
- No answer submission allowed during this phase
- **CRITICAL: Sync with live display** - ensure question appears instantly on main screen

**Phase 6: Answer Phase (30 Seconds)**
- Player submits answer within 30 seconds
- System checks correctness automatically
- **CRITICAL: Real-time sync** - result appears on main display immediately
- Update team scores across all devices simultaneously
- Show correct answer if wrong

**Phase 7: Next Player Rotation**
- Move to next player in same team
- Repeat phases 4-6
- Continue until all players in team have answered

**Phase 8: Next Team Transition**
- After team completes all turns, move to next team
- Repeat entire process for all 6 teams
- Continue until competition ends
